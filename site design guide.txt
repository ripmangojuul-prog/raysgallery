1. Core Aesthetic & Vibe

2. Color Palette
The work is almost exclusively black and grey with full tonal range — deep blacks, smooth mid-greys, and bright skin-tone highlights. The palette should reflect this.
css:root {
  /* Backgrounds */

## 5. Imagery & Layout Directives
### Portfolio Grid
| Directive | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| **Layout Type** | **Masonry grid** — 2 to 3 columns on desktop, single column on mobile. The varying subject sizes (full sleeves vs. small fine-line pieces) benefit from organic masonry flow rather than rigid grids. |
| **Gutter/Gap** | `4px–6px` — extremely tight gaps create a contact-sheet / proof-sheet aesthetic |
| **Image Treatment** | No borders, no rounded corners. Images should bleed edge-to-edge within their grid cell. Desaturate slightly by default (`filter: saturate(0.9)`) to normalize skin-tone variance. |
| **Lightbox** | On click, open a cinematic full-viewport lightbox with black overlay (`rgba(0,0,0,0.95)`) — let the piece breathe at maximum scale. |

Section padding: clamp(80px, 10vh, 160px) top and bottom
Max content width: 1400px centered
Hero section: Full viewport height with a single featured piece or subtle animated ink-wash texture background
Philosophy: Treat whitespace (in this case, blackspace) as a compositional element — it frames the work the way skin frames a tattoo

Additional Layout Notes

Navigation: Fixed top, minimal — logo left, 3–4 text links right, no hamburger on desktop. Use backdrop-filter: blur(10px) with semi-transparent var(--color-bg-primary).
Page transitions: Subtle fade-in (opacity 0 → 1 over 600ms) on route change.
Mobile: Single-column stack, full-bleed images, bottom sticky nav.
Loading: Skeleton placeholders in var(--color-bg-secondary) — no spinners.
